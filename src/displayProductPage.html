Product Information

This limited-edition Classic Candle celebrates LAFCO New York’s 30th anniversary in 2022. Created by company founder Jon Bresler to
commemorate the years he spent in Italy learning about the origins, craft, and art of perfumery, the fresh, succulent fragrance features
complementary notes of juicy and earthy fruits, including hints of Sicilian bergamot, ripe peach, and its namesake Paradiso fig – a renowned
Italian variety with vibrant green skin and vivid red pulp. The one-of-a-kind specially designed blown glass vessel combines whisps of clear and colored glass which render a delicate leafy pattern when illuminated.

    Key Benefits
  • Layers of the finest essential oil-based fragrances are combined with a clean-burning soy wax blend that is richly optimized for an effusive, long-lasting scent experience.
  • The artisanal blown glass container becomes an enduring keepsake object for your home once the candle is finished.

How to use

  • Light the candle on a heat-resistant surface within sight and away from drafts, children, and pets.
  • Trim the wick to 1/4" before every burn or when the wick "mushrooms" to maintain a clean burn (no trim is necessary for the first burn).
  • To prevent tunneling and to ensure an even burn, allow the melted pool of wax to reach the walls of the vessel every use.
  • Keep the wick centered at all times so the candle burns evenly.
  • Keep the wax pool free of matches, wick trimmings, and other debris.
